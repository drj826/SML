description:: The software shall enable a user to validate that
'infer-only' properties are ~~not~~ explicitly declared.  The ontology
specifies that certain properties are 'infer-only' properties,
meaning, they my only be implied by the application and may ~~not~~ be
explicitly declared in a document.  The purpose of disallowing
explicit declaration of certain properties is to prevent conflicting
property declarations.
