>>>problem.rq-000010

title:: Represent  a Paragraph

priority:: routine

part_of:: rq-000327

type:: data format

description:: The data format shall have the capability to represent
a paragraph.  A paragraph is an element of document [g:sml:structure],
a [g:sml:block] of [g:sml:text] that communicates an idea. The data
format representation of a paragraph must allow but not mandate
[g:sml:newline] characters within a paragraph.
